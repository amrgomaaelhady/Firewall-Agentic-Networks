## Insurance Star Rating System:

**One-Star Insurance:**
- Customer Support: Basic support, weekdays only (9AM-5PM)
- Deductible: Higher deductibles (20% of claim amount)
- Coverage: Essential coverage only, limited additional services
- Claims Process: Standard processing (15-30 business days)

**Two-Star Insurance:**
- Customer Support: Improved support, weekdays + limited weekends (8AM-6PM)
- Deductible: Moderate deductibles (15% of claim amount)
- Coverage: Essential coverage with some additional services
- Claims Process: Improved processing (10-20 business days)

**Three-Star Insurance:**
- Customer Support: Comprehensive support, weekdays + weekends (8AM-8PM)
- Deductible: Lower deductibles (10% of claim amount)
- Coverage: Comprehensive coverage, includes telemedicine services
- Claims Process: Efficient processing (5-15 business days)

**Four-Star Insurance:**
- Customer Support: High-quality 24/7 support
- Deductible: Low deductibles (5% of claim amount)
- Coverage: Extensive coverage, includes telemedicine, gym reimbursement, TV insurance up to €1000
- Claims Process: Streamlined processing (3-10 business days)

**Five-Star Insurance:**
- Customer Support: Premium 24/7 support with dedicated agents
- Deductible: Zero deductibles (0% of claim amount)
- Coverage: Extensive premium coverage, includes all services, TV insurance up to €5000
- Claims Process: Priority processing (1-5 business days)
