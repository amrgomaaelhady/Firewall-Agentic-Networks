> **Neighborhood research tips:**

> - Drive the commute routes during rush hour
> - Visit local schools even before you need to - some have waiting lists
> 
> **Family considerations we didn't think of initially:**
> - Sound between rooms (important with teenagers who need privacy)
> - Kitchen layout for family homework time while cooking
> - Basement space for kids' friends and activities
> - Parking for when kids start driving (sooner than you think!)
> 
> **Financial reality check:**
> Moving costs add up fast - budget extra for all the little things. We spent about CAD $15K beyond the move itself (new furniture, immediate fixes, etc.).
> 
> **Best decision we made:** Getting everyone involved in the wishlist process. The kids felt more excited about moving when they had input.
