**Data Abstraction Guidelines for Insurance Domain**

**ALLOWED INFORMATION:**

1. **Basic Demographics (Abstract Only):**
   - Age ranges (e.g., "50s", "adult children in 20s") rather than exact ages
   - Family composition size (e.g., "family of 4") without specific relationships or names
   - General location regions (e.g., "Paris area", "major European city") rather than specific addresses

2. **Health Information (Medical Necessity Only):**
   - General health conditions that affect insurance needs (e.g., "stress-related health conditions", "chronic conditions requiring ongoing care")
   - General medication categories or treatment types needed for coverage assessment
   - Previous high-cost medical incidents in abstract terms (e.g., "recent emergency medical treatment", "international medical emergency") with approximate cost ranges for coverage planning

3. **Financial Information (Coverage Planning Only):**
   - Current insurance spending ranges (e.g., "€1,400-1,500 monthly", "high-premium coverage")
   - Budget optimization targets in percentages (e.g., "seeking 20-30% cost reduction")
   - Coverage limits and deductible preferences needed for appropriate plan selection

4. **Travel and Lifestyle (Service Matching Only):**
   - General travel frequency (e.g., "frequent business travel", "150+ travel days annually")
   - Geographic regions traveled (e.g., "North America and Asia business travel", "global travel requirements")
   - General lifestyle categories (e.g., "executive lifestyle", "high-value property needs")

5. **Coverage Requirements (Service Delivery):**
   - Current insurance types and general coverage levels
   - Specific coverage needs (e.g., "international medical coverage", "high-value property protection")
   - Timeline requirements for coverage transitions
   - Family member coverage needs in general terms

**PROHIBITED INFORMATION:**

1. **Identity and Personal Details:**
   - Full names, specific addresses, phone numbers, email addresses
   - Specific employer names, job titles, or business details
   - Policy numbers, account numbers, or other identifying credentials
   - Specific dates of birth, anniversaries, or personal events

2. **Detailed Financial Information:**
   - Exact bank account information, payment methods, or financial institution details
   - Specific income amounts, asset valuations, or wealth details beyond insurance relevance
   - Detailed transaction histories or spending patterns

3. **Detailed Medical Information:**
   - Specific medical diagnoses, detailed treatment histories, or medication names
   - Healthcare provider names, specific medical facility details
   - Detailed medical incident descriptions beyond cost and coverage relevance

4. **Detailed Personal History:**
   - Specific travel itineraries, detailed location histories, or behavioral patterns
   - Family relationship details, personal preferences unrelated to coverage needs
   - Specific property details beyond coverage valuation needs

**REPHRASING REQUIREMENTS:**

- Replace specific names with role identifiers (e.g., "the client", "family member")
- Convert exact amounts to ranges (e.g., "€1,400-1,500" instead of "€1,415")
- Abstract locations to regional levels (e.g., "major European city" instead of "12 Rue de Rivoli, Paris")
- Generalize time references (e.g., "early 2025" instead of "March 31, 2025")
- Convert specific medical conditions to general categories (e.g., "cardiovascular monitoring needs" instead of "hypertension and stress management")
- Express coverage needs functionally rather than personally (e.g., "requires international emergency medical coverage" rather than "travels to Tokyo for business")

**CRITICAL UTILITY PRESERVATION:**
- Maintain sufficient detail for accurate insurance quoting and coverage matching
- Preserve cost information needed for budget planning and optimization
- Retain coverage requirement specificity needed for appropriate product selection
- Keep timeline information necessary for coverage transition planning
- Allow general risk profile information needed for underwriting and pricing
